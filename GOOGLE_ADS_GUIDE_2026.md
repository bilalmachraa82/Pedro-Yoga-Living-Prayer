## 1. Overview & Strategy Framework

### Why Google Ads for a Yoga Retreat in Sintra?

Yoga retreats are **high-intent, high-consideration purchases** (typically €200–€2000+). Potential guests actively search Google when planning retreats — unlike social media where discovery is passive. Google Ads captures people at the moment of intent.

### Google's 2026 Best Practice: AI Max + Smart Bidding

Google's official 2026 guidance (March 2026) emphasizes:

- **AI Max for Search campaigns**: One-click activation that enables search term matching, text customization, and final URL expansion. Google reports advertisers see **14% more conversions at similar CPA**, and up to **27% uplift** for campaigns previously using exact/phrase match.
- **Smart Bidding with value-based strategies**: Use Maximize Conversions with Target CPA or Maximize Conversion Value with Target ROAS.
- **Simplified account structure**: Fewer campaigns, broader targeting, let AI optimize at the query level.
- **Clear website content**: AI Max generates headlines from your landing page HTML — well-written page titles and content directly improve ad performance.

### Your Competitive Advantage: Sintra

Sintra is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its mystical energy, palaces, and nature. This is a **massive unique selling proposition (USP)**. Very few retreat destinations offer this combination of spiritual heritage, natural beauty, and proximity to a European capital (30 min from Lisbon).

## 2. Campaign Structure & Setup

### Recommended Campaign Architecture (Consolidated)

With a budget of €150–€250/month (approx. €5-€8/day), **consolidation is critical**. Smart Bidding requires 15-30 conversions per month per campaign to optimize effectively. Spreading the budget across multiple campaigns will starve the algorithm.

```
Account: Pedro Yoga Retreats
├── Campaign 1: Search — Retreat Bookings (EN) [85% budget]
│   └── Broad Match focus with aggressive Negative Keywords
│       (Targets high-intent international traffic)
│
└── Campaign 2: Remarketing Display [15% budget]
    └── Retarget website visitors (7-60 day windows)

*Note: Do not launch a separate Portuguese campaign or Performance Max until you scale the budget beyond €1500/month or have proven positive ROI on the core EN campaign.*
```

### Campaign Settings

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| **Campaign Type** | Search Network |
| **Bidding** | Maximize Conversions (Once you hit 15+ conversions/month, switch to Target CPA) |
| **Match Types** | **Broad Match** (Google's 2026 best practice, but REQUIRES strict negative keywords) |
| **Location** | Portugal + UK + Germany + France + Netherlands + Nordics (top yoga tourism markets) |
| **Languages** | English |
| **Ad Schedule** | Boost bids 20% during evenings/weekends (when people research retreats) |
| **Device** | All devices, +20% mobile bid adjustment (most retreat research happens on mobile) |

## 8. Google Business Profile Setup

### Why It's Essential

Google Business Profile (GBP) is **free** and directly impacts Google Ads performance:

- **Location extensions** in ads show your address, driving directions
- **Local pack rankings** — appears in Google Maps results for "yoga retreat near me"
- **Reviews displayed in ads** — Google can show your review rating directly in Search ads
- **Trust signal** — verified business with reviews increases ad click-through rate
- **Free clicks** — GBP generates organic visibility alongside paid ads

### Setup Steps

1. Go to **business.google.com/add**
2. Enter business name: "Pedro Yoga" (or official business name)
3. Select category: **Primary: "Yoga Retreat Center"** / **Secondary: "Yoga Studio", "Wellness Center"**
4. Enter full address in Sintra
5. Add service area: Sintra + Lisbon (30 km radius)
6. Verify via postcard, phone, or video verification
7. Complete all fields:

| Field | What to Add |
|-------|-------------|
| **Business Name** | Pedro Yoga (exact legal name — no keyword stuffing) |
| **Categories** | Yoga Retreat Center (primary), Yoga Studio, Wellness Center |
| **Address** | Full Sintra address |
| **Phone** | Portuguese phone number |
| **Website** | Your retreat website URL |
| **Hours** | Operating hours (or "By appointment" / retreat dates) |

- Reviews appear directly in your Google Ads (automatically by Google)

## 9. Remarketing Strategies

### Why Remarketing is Critical for Retreats

Yoga retreats have a **7–45 day decision cycle**. Most visitors won't book on the first visit. Remarketing keeps Pedro Yoga top-of-mind during their research phase.

### Remarketing Audience Segments

| Audience | Window | Message |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **All website visitors** | 7–30 days | Brand reminder + Sintra imagery |
| **Retreat page viewers** | 7–30 days | "Still thinking about Sintra? Check our upcoming dates" |
| **Form abandoners** (started but didn't submit) | 3–14 days | "Complete your inquiry — spots are filling up" |
| **Past retreat guests** | 90–365 days | "Come back to Sintra — new retreat experiences await" |
| **Blog/content readers** | 14–60 days | Educational content + soft CTA |

### Remarketing Campaign Setup

#### Display Remarketing Campaign
- **Format**: Responsive Display Ads + custom images of Sintra
- **Frequency cap**: 3 impressions/day per user (don't overwhelm)
- **Bidding**: Maximize Conversions, no Target CPA (let it optimize)
- **Exclusions**: Converters (people who booked) — exclude for 90 days
- **Budget**: €2.50–3.50/day

#### Remarketing Lists for Search Ads (RLSA)
- Apply remarketing audiences to your Search campaigns as **observations**
- Set **+20% bid adjustment** for remarketing audiences (they're warmer leads)
- Create a separate "RLSA-only" campaign for high-intent keywords with remarketing list membership required

#### Email Remarketing (Free — via Google Ads Customer Match)
- Upload email list of past retreat guests and inquiries
- Target with special "return guest" offers
- Create a "Similar Audiences" segment from this list

## 13. Bilingual Strategy (PT/EN)

### Why Separate Campaigns?

- **Different keywords**: Portuguese search behavior differs from English
- **Different ad copy**: Tone and messaging vary by culture
- **Different competition levels**: Portuguese keywords typically have lower CPC (€0.30–€0.80 vs €1.00–€3.00 for English)
- **Different audiences**: Portuguese searchers may be local/day retreat seekers vs. English speakers looking for multi-day retreats
- **Budget control**: Allocate independently based on performance

### Landing Pages

- Create **separate landing pages** for PT and EN
- Use `/pt/` and `/en/` URL structure, or separate subdomains
- Each page's HTML title, meta description, and H1 must be in the matching language
- Google's AI Max generates language-matched headlines from the landing page

### Cultural Considerations

| Aspect | English-Speaking Audience | Portuguese Audience |
|--------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| **Tone** | Transformative, spiritual journey | Bem-estar, relaxamento, saúde |
| **Key motivator** | "Escape", "transform", "disconnect" | "Relaxar", "cuidar de si", "equilíbrio" |
| **Price sensitivity** | Medium-High (willing to pay for experience) | Higher sensitivity — emphasize value |
| **Retreat length** | 3–7 days preferred | Weekend/1 day popular |
| **Booking lead time** | 4–12 weeks | 1–4 weeks |
| **USP emphasis** | Sintra's mystique, UNESCO, "Europe's hidden gem" | Proximidade, qualidade, experiência única |

## Google Ads Policy Notes for Wellness/Yoga

Google has specific policies around **healthcare and medicines**. To avoid ad disapprovals:

- **DO NOT** claim yoga cures/treats specific medical conditions (depression, anxiety as medical diagnosis)
- **DO NOT** make unsubstantiated health claims
- **DO** use language like "support your wellbeing", "complement your wellness journey"
- **DO** get any required certifications (Google may require healthcare certification for some wellness terms in EU)
- Avoid words like "healing", "cure", "treatment", "therapy" in ad copy
- Use "wellness", "mindfulness", "relaxation", "self-care" instead
